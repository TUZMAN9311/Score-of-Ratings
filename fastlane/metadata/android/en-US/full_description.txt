Score of Ratings is a simple and fast calculator for school and university students. It helps you instantly calculate your grade point average (GPA) and see your final grade right away — without extra math or stress.

What's inside:

Instant calculation: the app automatically calculates the arithmetic mean and the rounded grade.

Clear feedback: add your grades and immediately see how the result changes — no complex formulas needed.

Works everywhere: the interface adapts to any screen, from budget smartphones to flagships.

Error protection: the app won't crash if you make an accidental typo — you will always get a correct result.

Full privacy: no internet required, no registration needed. The app does not save any personal data.
